# OPS Framework - User Manual

## 1. Getting Started
To access the OPS Framework, navigate to your Odoo instance URL and log in with your credentials.

## 2. Daily Operations

### Sales Orders (SO)
- Navigate to **Sales > Orders**.
- Click **Create** to start a new sale order.
- Add products, set quantities, and confirm the order.

### PDC Management
- Located under the **Accounting** menu.
- **PDC Receivable**: Manage checks received from customers.
- **PDC Payable**: Manage checks issued to vendors.
- Workflows: Move checks from 'Draft' to 'Deposited/Issued' and finally 'Cleared'.

### Approvals
- The **Approval Dashboard** provides a bird's eye view of pending requests.
- Take action on requests based on your persona and authority level.

## 3. Dashboard Usage
- **Executive Dashboard**: High-level financial KPIs.
- **Operational Dashboard**: Real-time tracking of orders and deliveries.
- **Approval Dashboard**: Centralized governance and SLA tracking.

## 4. Troubleshooting
- If a record is not visible, check your access rights and filters.
- For technical issues, contact the system administrator.
